What makes us special?

Varassiar Chips stands out with its commitment to authentic flavors and wholesome ingredients. Using locally-sourced produce and traditional recipes, we craft chips that capture the essence of home cooking. Our unique production process preserves natural textures and tastes, resulting in a satisfyingly crisp snack. Free from artificial additives and preservatives, Varassiar Chips offer a pure, unadulterated chip experience. With a range of distinctive flavors inspired by local cuisine, each bag delivers a taste of genuine craftsmanship and culinary heritage.

No preservatives!

Discover the pure, natural goodness of our preservative-free chips. We believe in delivering authentic flavors without compromising on health. Our chips are crafted using only fresh, high-quality ingredients and traditional cooking methods. By skipping artificial preservatives, we ensure you taste the real essence of our carefully selected potatoes and seasonings. Enjoy a satisfyingly crisp snack that's not only delicious but also free from unnecessary additives.

We use orginal coconut oil!

Varassiar Chips takes pride in using genuine coconut oil for their crispy creations. This natural choice not only enhances flavor but also offers a healthier alternative to conventional cooking oils. By carefully controlling oil usage, they achieve the perfect balance of taste and texture without excess grease. The result is a light, satisfying snack that captures the essence of traditional chip-making while catering to modern health-conscious consumers.
